# ASO Keyword Research — TaskFlow iOS
**Version**: 1.0
**Last Updated**: 2026-03-30
**Methodology**: Competitor analysis, category top charts, search suggestion scraping, volume/difficulty scoring (1–10 scale)

---

## Executive Summary

TaskFlow competes in the **Productivity** category — one of the App Store's most contested spaces. The winning keyword strategy focuses on:

1. **High-intent, specific queries** where users are actively looking for a Todoist / Things 3 alternative.
2. **iOS-native feature terms** (Siri tasks, widget planner) where TaskFlow has a genuine moat.
3. **Long-tail persona terms** with lower competition and strong conversion intent (freelancer planner, student organizer).
