## App Store Description (4,000 char limit)

---

**Stop juggling apps. Start finishing what matters.**

TaskFlow is the task manager built for the way you actually work — fast to capture, smart about priorities, and deeply woven into iOS so it disappears into your day.

Whether you're a freelancer managing client projects, a professional keeping work from bleeding into life, or a student staying ahead of deadlines, TaskFlow gives you one calm, focused place for everything on your plate.

---

**⚡ CAPTURE IN SECONDS, NOT STEPS**

A task idea shouldn't require three taps, two menus, and a loading spinner. TaskFlow's Quick Add sheet opens instantly — title and done. Add details when you have them.

• Capture from the Lock Screen with a single tap
• "Hey Siri, add [task] to TaskFlow" — hands-free capture
• Add from anywhere with Siri Shortcuts

---

**🎯 YOUR DAY, FOCUSED**

The Today View does the thinking for you. Every morning, TaskFlow surfaces exactly what needs your attention — overdue tasks, priority items, and what's due today — sorted by time and importance.

• See your top priorities without manual sorting
• Morning Digest notification to start the day ready
• Visual progress bar to keep momentum going

---

**📁 ORGANIZE YOUR WAY**

From a single Inbox to structured projects with custom sections — TaskFlow scales from a quick errand list to a multi-client project board.

• Unlimited projects with custom icons and colors
• Sections inside projects (To Do → In Progress → Done)
• Tags for cross-project filtering
• Drag-and-drop to reorder everything

---

**🔔 REMINDERS THAT WORK**

Rich iOS notifications with "Complete" and "Snooze 1h" actions right from the lock screen. Never open the app to confirm a task again.

• Time-based reminders (set multiple per task)
• Location-based reminders: trigger when you arrive or leave (Pro)
• Daily Digest — your day summarized every morning

---

**📱 BUILT FOR APPLE. REALLY.**

TaskFlow doesn't bolt iOS features on as an afterthought. It's built around them.

• Interactive Home Screen Widgets (small, medium, large)
• Lock Screen widget showing today's task count
• Siri Shortcuts — automate your entire workflow
• iCloud sync across iPhone and iPad — instant, silent, reliable
• Full offline support — works everywhere, syncs when ready
• Dark Mode, Dynamic Type, VoiceOver, Reduce Motion

**🔒 YOUR DATA IS YOURS**

All tasks are stored in your personal iCloud (CloudKit private database). TaskFlow uses no third-party ad trackers. No personal data leaves your devices except to your own iCloud account.
